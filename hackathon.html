tracks

choose a track to participate in:

  • community good -

    the community good track in the helium hackathon is focused on projects that benefit the public good by leveraging the capabilities of the helium network. the aim of this track is to encourage participants to use their skills to make a positive impact on their community, whether it is related to environmental conservation, healthcare initiatives, or other areas that can benefit from the use of wireless technology.

    participants in this track are encouraged to develop innovative and creative solutions that address important community needs using the Helium network. projects in this track could focus on areas such as air and water quality monitoring, waste reduction, energy conservation, and other sustainability initiatives.

    the goal of the community good track is to showcase how the helium network can be leveraged to create meaningful change in communities around the world, and to inspire participants to think creatively about how technology can be used to address important social and environmental issues.

  • iot solutions -

    the iot solution track of the helium hackathon focuses on projects that leverage helium's decentralized wireless network to deploy personal or scalable solutions using lorawan sensors. participants in this track may develop a wide range of iot solutions, such as smart home devices, environmental sensors, industrial monitoring systems, and more. The track offers a lot of flexibility for participants to showcase their creativity and innovative thinking in developing IoT solutions that make use of Helium's network capabilities.

  • helium xnfts / dapps on solana -

    the helium xnfts on solana track focuses on the creation of unique and innovative non-fungible tokens (nft) that leverage helium's decentralized wireless network and solana's smart program capabilities. the goal is to create decentralized apps (dapps), executable NFTs, or other utilities or programs that provide functionality to dapps that improve user experience for the helium community.

    this can include but is not limited to solutions that enhance the functionality of the helium wallet, improve the tracking and management of devices on the helium network, or enhance the accessibility and usability of the helium ecosystem as a whole. the track is open to developers and creators who want to explore the intersection of helium and solana and build cutting-edge solutions that push the boundaries of what's possible in the world of decentralized applications.

deadlines for submissions: may 19

participants will have 6 weeks to network, create, launch, observe, learn, and repeat as necessary! we will provide networking, mentorship, and educational resources over the 6 weeks to help ensure a successful launch for each of our contestants. you can participate from anywhere in the world, but you must be present to win!

judging criteria

projects will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

  • innovation: how creative and original is the idea?
  • impact: what is the potential impact of the project?
  • technical execution: how well is the idea implemented technically?
  • presentation: how well is the project presented and explained?
